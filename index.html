Timeless Wisdom. Heroic Legacies

Journey through India’s spiritual heart and its most courageous lives - explore a curated collection of stories spanning the depths of our religion, the bravery of India’s freedom fighters, and the spark of inspiration by Om Prakash Agrawal.

Discover the Books

Published Works

Shiv & Shiva

Short stories compilation of eternal tales about Lord Shiva and Parvati which reimagines their journey, exploring their trials, triumphs, and the divine dance of the universe.

Devi Kunti

This book delves into the depth of Kunti’s profound wisdom, unraveling the meaning behind her request and the spiritual essence of surrender and devotion. A thought-provoking exploration of faith, destiny, and divine grace.

Bharat Ki Vibhutiyan

A stirring collection of true stories from India's freedom struggle — both celebrated and forgotten. This book brings to life the courage, sacrifice, and resilience of known heroes and unsung warriors who helped shape the nation's destiny.

Prerna

Prerna (Inspiration) book is a heartfelt collection of short stories and poems that inspire, uplift, and awaken the human spirit. Each piece reflects simple moments of life, yet carries profound lessons of hope, courage, and resilience. A companion for all ages, it invites readers to journey inward, embrace life’s challenges with grace, and nurture the light that shines within.

Hastinapur

Hastinapur is a comprehensive and essential guide to the monumental epic of the Mahabharata, anchored by the divine presence of Shri Krishna. This definitive volume illuminates the complex motivations of all the key characters—from the noble Pandavas to the ambitious Kauravas. This work serves as the perfect companion for anyone seeking profound insight into the epic's drama, philosophy, and enduring spiritual legacy.

Ram Kahani

Ram Kahani brings the life of Lord Ram to your fingertips, in an accessible and engaging style with illustrations in each chapters, focussing on the heart of his journey. From his playful childhood in Ayodhya to the high-stakes battles in Lanka, this book explores the adventures and emotions that shaped the "Maryada Purushottam."

Meet the Author

A Journey of Grace


Neither a poet nor a writer, the author considers himself merely a humble servant of Lord Krishna. Inspired by devotion, this book marks his first attempt to express his reverence through words.

Though a student of Science, his heart is deeply drawn to spirituality.

He humbly invites readers to share their valuable feedback, hoping to refine and improve his writing on this sacred journey

May you find encouragement, healing, and a deeper connection to the divine within these pages.
